info About the Initiative

Transforming Governance with
AI-Driven Inclusivity

नैत्रम् (Naitram) is an innovative, AI-driven grievance system designed to transform citizen interaction with governance. It eliminates language and literacy barriers by enabling voice-first multilingual complaint submission in local languages and dialects.

Leveraging Generative AI and a Dialect-Agnostic Voice Engine, the system converts informal speech into structured reports, intelligently routes them to government departments, and auto-flags hazards for immediate prioritization.

Our mission is to reduce administrative backlogs and provide faster, inclusive, and transparent resolution for all, making governance accessible to everyone.
